About Our Voting Platform

Welcome to EPICS – a next-generation secure online voting platform that empowers democracy with cutting-edge technology.

Our mission is to streamline and secure the voting process using Artificial Intelligence, Biometric Authentication (Face & Fingerprint), and Blockchain to ensure transparency, privacy, and efficiency.

🔐 Key Features

  • ✔️ Face and Fingerprint Verification
  • ✔️ Tamper-proof Blockchain Vote Storage
  • ✔️ Voter Identity Proof using Government Issued PDFs
  • ✔️ Decentralized and Transparent Vote Counting
  • ✔️ Real-time Admin Analytics and Region-wise Tallying

With ElectraChain, every vote is secure, anonymous, and verified – bringing the future of digital voting today.
